# Virly Personal Loan Packages

Virly offers three personal loan packages. All amounts are in ILS.

## Starter Loan
- Amount: ₪2,000 – ₪15,000
- Term: 6 to 24 months
- Annual interest rate (APR): 9.9%
- Eligibility: verified Virly account, at least 3 months of account history
- No early-repayment fee

## Flex Loan
- Amount: ₪15,001 – ₪75,000
- Term: 12 to 48 months
- Annual interest rate (APR): 7.5%
- Eligibility: verified account, 6 months of history, positive average balance
- One free repayment holiday per 12 months

## Premium Loan
- Amount: ₪75,001 – ₪250,000
- Term: 24 to 84 months
- Annual interest rate (APR): 5.9%
- Eligibility: verified account, 12 months of history, salary deposit into Virly
- Dedicated loan advisor and a Jitsi video consultation before approval

## How to apply
Customers apply through the Virly app. Approval decisions are typically returned
within two business days. A soft credit check is performed and does not affect
the customer's credit score.
