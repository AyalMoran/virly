# Account Eligibility and Verification Policy

## Who can open a Virly account
- Individuals aged 18 or older.
- A valid email address and phone number are required at registration.
- Email verification must be completed before the account can send money.

## Verification levels
- **Basic**: email verified. Can receive money and view the dashboard.
- **Verified**: personal details (legal name, date of birth, address) on file.
  Required to send transfers and to apply for any loan package.

## Loan eligibility summary
A customer must hold a Verified account to apply for a loan. Minimum account
history depends on the package: 3 months for Starter, 6 months for Flex, and
12 months plus a salary deposit for Premium. See the Personal Loan Packages
document for full terms.
