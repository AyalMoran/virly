# Transfer, Limits and Fees Policy

## Transfer limits
- Default per-transfer limit: ₪500
- Default daily transfer limit: ₪1,000
- Limits can be raised for verified customers with personal details on file.

## Fees
- Transfers between Virly users: free.
- Currency conversion (ILS, USD, EUR): a 0.5% FX margin applies on the live rate.
- There is no monthly account fee on personal accounts.

## Early repayment
Personal loans may be repaid early at any time. The Starter and Flex packages
carry no early-repayment fee. The Premium package has no early-repayment fee
after the first 12 months; before that, a 1% fee on the remaining principal
applies.

## Refunds and reversals
A transfer can be reversed only if the recipient has not yet accepted the funds.
Once accepted, reversals require both parties to agree.
