How it was authored

The knowledge base (38 policy/loan docs) is Drive-sourced and already synced into pgvector; sourceRef is the opaque Drive fileId. Each document's title + content was read from the store, and one customer-style question was written per document whose answer lives in that document, paired with its exact sourceRef. Questions are grounded in real content - never fabricated, never widened to whatever was retrieved.
