Playground, explained

A playground is a per-branch, self-contained HTML explainer under docs/playgrounds/<branch-slug>/ (slug = branch with / -> --). It's the PR description's interactive big sibling. Enforced advisory-only, twice: a local Claude hook on push/PR, and now the CI playground job. New docs/playgrounds/README.md is the canonical answer; docs/testing.md and the local .claude/CLAUDE.md describe the expanded matrix.
